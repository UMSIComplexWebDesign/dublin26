# Accessibility review: Natural History Museum Part 1

**Observer:** Tasnimah Uddin  
**Date:** 2026-05-11  
**Overall compliance:** 43% of rated items met (6 met, 2 not met, 6 partial, 3 N/A)

 
## Audio/visual & interactives

- ⚠️ All videos and audio programs are captioned or have printed scripts — **Partial**
  > No captions on videos
- ➖ Silent audiovisuals labelled to confirm no audio is being missed — **N/A**
- ❌ Audio description available for image-based content — **Not met**
- ⚠️ Interactive controls reachable from a wheelchair (max. 48 in. height) — **Partial**
  > There are no controls but there were a few headphones for listening to audios
- ➖ Controls usable one-handed — no tight grip or twisting required — **N/A**
  > no controls displayed for audio visuals
- ➖ Touch targets at least 3 in. in diameter — **N/A**
- ⚠️ Instructions provided in both print and audio formats — **Partial**
- ✅ No furniture or objects blocking access to interactives — **Met**
- ❌ Assistive listening system available in programming spaces — **Not met**

 

## Seating & furniture

- ✅ Seating provided in every gallery (or immediately adjacent) — **Met**
  > seats were in every floor, and exhibit. along with benches
- ✅ At least 50% of seats are accessible — **Met**
- ⚠️ Seats have both back and arm support; seat height 17–19 in. — **Partial**
  > no arm support
- ⚠️ Space for wheelchair users beside fixed seating — **Partial**
- ✅ Seats clearly visible — contrast with floors and walls — **Met**
- ✅ Seats do not block pathways or interactive controls — **Met**
- ✅ Display cases have cane-detectable lower edges (at or below 27 in.) — **Met**
